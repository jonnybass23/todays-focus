Privacy & Terms

Last updated: June 2026

The short version

We store what you type. We don't track you, sell your data, show ads, or share anything with third parties. Your data lives on this server and nowhere else.

What we store

  • Your username — visible to admins of this server
  • Your password — stored as a bcrypt hash only, never in plaintext
  • Your cards, boards, and focus history — everything you create
  • Your theme preference and timezone
  • Recovery codes — stored as SHA-256 hashes only, never plaintext
  • A session cookie — set when you sign in (see Cookies below)

What we don't collect

  • Email address — there is no email system
  • IP addresses — used only for rate-limiting (held in memory, never written to disk or logs)
  • Browser or device fingerprints
  • Analytics or usage tracking of any kind — no page-view counters, no event tracking
  • Third-party tracking cookies or pixels
  • Payment information — the service is free

Cookies

One cookie is set when you sign in: a signed session token. It is httpOnly (JavaScript cannot read it), scoped to this site only, and automatically cleared when you log out or after 30 days. No tracking cookies are set at any point.

Third parties

Your card data never leaves this server. The one external dependency is Google Fonts — the Inter typeface is loaded from fonts.googleapis.com when you visit. Google Fonts does not set cookies and does not log IP addresses persistently (Google's Fonts privacy FAQ).

If you prefer to block this request, a system font will be used as fallback and nothing else changes. All other assets (CSS, JavaScript, icons) are served directly from this server.

Your data rights

  • Download: export a full JSON copy of your boards and history from Account → "Download my boards & history"
  • Delete: contact the server administrator to have your account and all associated data permanently removed
  • Portability: the exported JSON is human-readable and contains everything stored about you

Security

  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (10 rounds) — even the server operator cannot read them
  • Sessions are signed JWTs stored in httpOnly cookies
  • Login and registration are rate-limited per IP; repeated failures lock out an account temporarily
  • When served over HTTPS, an HSTS header is sent to prevent downgrade attacks

Terms of use

  • Don't abuse the service — automated mass signup, scripted card flooding, or attempts to circumvent rate limits may result in account suspension
  • The service is provided as-is, with no uptime guarantee
  • The server administrator may delete accounts that violate these terms
  • You own your data; we have no rights to use it beyond storing it for you

Contact

This service is operated by the server administrator. For questions about your data or these terms, contact them directly. For issues with the software, the project is open-source under the MIT licence.
