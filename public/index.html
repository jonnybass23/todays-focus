How it works

⚡

Capture in seconds

Type "email Steve tomorrow 3pm #work !!" and it files the task with the right date, tag and priority — no menus.

🔔

Never miss a thing

Add due dates, repeats and reminders. Get a push notification on your phone when something's due — even with the app closed.

Stay focused

Everything lives in the sidebar, but the Focus Zone keeps one thing front and centre — so a full plate never becomes noise.
